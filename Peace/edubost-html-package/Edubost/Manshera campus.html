Welcome to

The PEACE Group of Schools & Colleges!

Welcome to The Peace Group of Colleges – Mansehra Campus At The Peace Group, we take pride in being one of the fastest-growing educational networks with 27 campuses nationwide. Our Mansehra Campus stands as a symbol of excellence and commitment, dedicated to nurturing future leaders ready to take on the challenges of the 21st century. Founded over two decades ago, The Peace Group was established with a clear mission: to empower generations with modern education aligned with international standards. We aim to provide a dynamic learning environment where students grow intellectually, morally, and socially. At the Mansehra Campus, we believe that every student is gifted with unique talents and potential. Our teaching approach focuses on identifying individual strengths, fostering creativity, and building a strong foundation through skill-based and concept-driven education. Join The Peace Group of Colleges – Mansehra Campus, and be part of a legacy where education meets excellence.

  • A vibrant and supportive school & college community
  • Flexible learning – Available in three convenient time zones
  • Leadership programs that empower students to lead and inspire

Dear Students and Parents,
