Welcome to

The PEACE Group of Schools & Colleges!

We welcome you to the rising educational network "The Peace Group". We are striving to be the best with our 27 Campuses.
The Peace Schools and Colleges aim to prepare their students for the 21st-century challenges with established international standards. Twenty years earlier, the Peace was founded with a mission to equip the coming generations with the curriculum to compete with the contemporary world.
We believe that every student is skillful and has unique qualities, that help us to know the nature. Because of this, our way of teaching is based on the identification of nature of subjects and the development of personal skills. This greatly helps in strengthening their foundation.

Dear Students and Parents,
At The Peace Group of Schools and Colleges, we believe education is the foundation for a brighter future. Our mission is to nurture young minds in an environment that values learning, respect, and personal growth. We encourage every student to embrace curiosity, develop strong character, and strive for excellence in all pursuits.
Together, let us foster a culture of peace, tolerance, and positive change—both within our school and in the wider community. I invite you to join us on this journey of learning and transformation, where every child is empowered to reach their full potential.
