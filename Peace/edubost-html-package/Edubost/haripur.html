Welcome to

The PEACE Group of Schools & Colleges!

We are proud to welcome you to The Peace Group, one of Pakistan’s rapidly growing educational networks with over 27 campuses nationwide. Our Haripur Campus stands as a beacon of quality education, character-building, and future-focused learning. Established over two decades ago, The Peace Group was founded with a clear vision: to prepare future generations with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape. Today, we continue that mission with passion, innovation, and unwavering commitment. At the Haripur Campus, we believe that every student holds unique potential. Our educational approach centers on recognizing individual strengths, nurturing talents, and encouraging critical thinking. We go beyond textbooks—developing personal skills, emotional intelligence, and leadership qualities to ensure a strong foundation for lifelong success.

  • A vibrant, inclusive, and supportive academic community
  • Education accessible across three time zones
  • Student Leadership Programs that shape tomorrow’s leaders

meet the Dedicated principal to Success

Dear Students and Respected Parents,
It is an honor and a privilege to welcome you to The Peace Group of Colleges, Haripur Campus an institution where academic rigor meets holistic development. At our campus, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge it's about shaping minds, building character, and preparing young individuals to meet the challenges of the modern world with confidence and integrity. Our aim is to provide a nurturing environment that empowers students to become innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible citizens.
We take pride in recognizing and developing the unique strengths of each student. Through personalized learning, modern teaching methods, and a strong emphasis on ethical values, we strive to help every learner unlock their full potential. As we continue to grow as a vibrant educational community in Haripur, I invite you to walk with us on this inspiring journey one that is rooted in excellence, driven by purpose, and committed to making a meaningful difference
Warm regards,
